Website Design & Creation

Role: Website Designer • Tools: Figma, Visual Studio Code, HTML • Time: 6 weeks

Purpose: To design and develop a website from start to finish. The website will also serve to display other projects.

Audience

The first step in web design is to have a clear picture of the audience and what message is being told. This help to ensure that the website serves it's purpose. In the case of my website, my audience are students who are also insterested in looking into different information systems careers. I geared this website toward outlining the background information and steps of a few different careers.
