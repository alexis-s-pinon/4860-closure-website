Business Analysis
The Math Learning Center

Role: Business Analyst • Tools: Draw.io, • Time: 5 weeks

Purpose: To gain experience in business analysis by working with a local small business to identify and create a solution to an existing bottleneck in operations.

Approach


Understanding the Business


Interview Hightlights

What is the main purpose of the business? To educate kids and help them improve their skills in math.

What duties do you handle as the center director? I handle the kid’s education, communication, progress updates, booking and handling meetings, staffing, training, inventory, and outreach.

Where do you experience slow downs in your daily work? Emails that ask hard questions, progress updates, and managing the small tasks that keep the center running.

What do you feel is the hardest part of your responsibilities? Making sure we don’t run out of things and making sure the flow of operations is there. Not falling behind on communications is also tough.

What do you wish you could magically make happen? Probably the progress and teacher emails. Also the inventory monitoring, that takes a long time to check everything.

What are your current processeses for tracking inventory and communication? For inventory, I just try to keep track in my head. Since it takes so much time to check everything individually, it feels like a waste of time to have an employee check it when they could spend that time teaching a student. I try to just kind of know around when things runs out. But then it happens where I’m off and we run out of something. As for emails, currently I wait until about 10 emails of the same type pile up and then do them all at once. I write up customized messages for each individual email and attach the relevant files.

Career Insight: This project taught me that clarity in communication is as critical as technical skill for a Business Analyst.

Objective


After discussion with key stakeholders, we determined the objective of this project would be to create a better system for monitoring inventory.

Scope

The scope of this project includes creating a simple, relatively stand-alone solution to maintaining inventory within the center.

*Budget: Priority is to create something with existing tools to avoid additional costs.

Requirements

• Must integrate with existing system
• Must be simple enough that any employee can do it with minimal training
• Shouldn't significantly increase operational costs, if possible
• Should prompt action rather than requiring attention to be completed

Timeline

Implementation

After creating the webpage for my project, I integrated it with the business's Google accounts so that it would work seamlessly with existing operations. An important requirement of the stakeholders was that my solution be as simple, intuitive, and integrated as possible.

Value

This new inventory system helped to streamline operations for the business. With this in place, they now have a system for identifying when items are running low and how to report that information to the appropriate person. This has saved significant costs in labor hours as well as reduced the impact of missing supplies.
